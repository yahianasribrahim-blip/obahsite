// Who We Are //

We take care of your best friend

Ottawa Barrhaven Animal Hospital has been built with the latest technology and veterinary equipment to provide full extensive care to your furry friends. We believe that we must match our excellent services with competitive affordable prices to help our community have an equal access to veterinary services.

Ottawa Barrhaven Animal Hospital is a fully equipped veterinary hospital accredited by the College of Veterinarians of Ontario (CVO). This licensing organization conducts regular on-site inspections of our premises to ensure that specific professional standards are met — including equipment, sterilization, patient record keeping, pharmacy controls and our medical reference library.

Our team is committed to treating every patient like family, with honest communication and evidence-based medicine at the core of everything we do.

// CVO Accredited //

Trusted, Certified, Local

As a College of Veterinarians of Ontario (CVO) accredited facility, we undergo regular on-site inspections to ensure we meet — and exceed — the standards expected of a modern veterinary hospital.

That means up-to-date equipment, strict sterilization protocols, careful patient record keeping, and a robust pharmacy and medical reference library to back every decision we make.
