The People Behind the Care

Experienced, compassionate, and dedicated — these are the people your pets will meet when they walk through our doors.

Veterinarian

Dr. Nasr

Dr. Nasr brings years of clinical experience and a calm, reassuring approach to every appointment. He's focused on honest medicine and clear communication with the families he serves.

Veterinarian

Dr. Abdou

Dr. Abdou is dedicated to evidence-based veterinary care and treating every patient like family. He takes the time to explain every diagnosis so pet owners can make confident decisions.

Veterinarian

Dr. Kamat

Dr. Kamat focuses on preventive care and early detection — helping pets live longer, healthier lives. Patients' families often comment on the patience and care he brings to every exam.

Vet Assistant

Sabrina P.

Sabrina assists with everything from wellness exams to surgical recovery. She's known around the clinic for her gentle handling and the way she remembers every pet by name.

Vet Assistant

Cassi

Cassi is a Veterinary Assistant with a true love for animals. At home she is kept busy by her husky, Sasha. Outside the clinic, she enjoys snowboarding or playing board games with friends.

Vet Assistant

Alana J.

Alana works closely with the veterinary team to keep every visit running smoothly. She has a way of putting nervous pets — and nervous owners — at ease.
